Community Guidelines

⚠️ Important: Respected flat owners are humbly urged to ensure their tenants abide by all guidelines, as they share equal responsibility in upholding the harmony, cleanliness, and safety of our shared living environment.

Community Guidelines List

▸ Financial Contributions:
  • Please set a reminder to pay your Monthly Maintenance Charge (MMC) by 15th of each month to avoid incurring a late fine of Rs. 50.00.
  • IF MMC IS UNPAID FOR TWO MONTHS OR MORE, ALL MMC – RELATED SERVICES WILL BE TEMPORARILY SUSPENDED UNTIL DUES ARE CLEARED.
▸ Waste Management:
  • Dispose of household waste only in the morning hours, before our housekeeping team begins the collection process.
  • Please refrain from discarding any waste—such as cigarette butts, liquor bottles, sachets, food remnants, or packaging materials—in shared spaces including lobbies, stairwells, gardens, terraces, or the rear areas of the building. Let’s treat shared spaces with care and respect.
▸ Tenant & Relative Regulations:
  • The flat owner must ensure that all dues to the Association are paid before renting out their flat.
  • Submission of duly filled PRESCRIBED TENANCY FORM along with other supporting documents is MANDATORY at least 3 days in advance before allowing any tenant into the allotted flat.
  • IF If a tenant intends to vacate the flat, the owner must provide prior written consent to the President or Secretary.
  • To ensure a smooth transition and minimize inconvenience to neighbours, all move-in and move-out activities must be completed during DAYLIGHT hours.
  • THESE RULES ARE EQUALLY RELEVANT BEFORE ALLOWING RELATIVES AS WELL.
▸ Respect Noise Levels:
  • Avoid loud music and noise especially during night hours.
  • Be considerate of your neighbors.
▸ Alterations / Repairs / Installation Protocol:
  • Please notify the Association at least 3 days in prior to commencing any renovation / repair / installation (e.g., AC units, Dish antennas, etc.) work.
  • Work Window: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM || Quiet Window: 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM (no noisy work permitted)
  • Ensure no work causes disruption or inconvenience to neighbouring residents.
  • Dispose of any debris or unserviceable materials within seven (7) days of production.
  • Kindly ensure no work compromises the structural safety or stability of the building.
▸ Security Protocols:
  • Always inform the security guard if you are expecting visitors or deliveries, and ensure they sign in at the gate.
  • To avoid manual intervention, enjoy this facility by installing NoBrokerHood App.
  • Report any suspicious activity to the Security or Association Office immediately.
▸ Use Common Facilities Respectfully:
  • Please turn off lights of common areas when not in use and make an effort to conserve water to promote sustainability.
  • Familiarize yourself with the locations of fire extinguishers, emergency exits, and designated assembly points to ensure a swift and safe response in case of an emergency.
▸ Parking Etiquette:
  • Please park only in your designated spot. Visitor vehicles must use visitor parking only.
  • Do not block driveways or emergency exits.
  • Parking beyond 24 hours requires prior EC approval.
  • Speed limit within the society: 10 km/hr—for the safety of all, especially children and the elderly.
▸ Pet Regulations:
  • Pets should not be left unattended on the terrace or in open areas within the towers.
  • Please ensure pets do not defecate on terraces, stairwells, or inside the towers to avoid discomfort for fellow residents.
  • Feeding pets inside the building premises or on staircases is discouraged to maintain cleanliness.
  • Be mindful of noise created by pets, such as barking, especially during night hours.
