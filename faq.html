Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Avishikta - I Type-A RWA.

How do I book the community hall?
You can book the community hall by filling out the Community Hall Reservation Form available on the Download Center page and submitting it to the management office.
Where can I download the maintenance payment forms?
Maintenance payment forms are available for download in the Download Center under the PDF category.
What are the office hours for the management office?
The management office is open from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
How can I contact the security service?
Security service contact numbers are listed on the Emergency Contacts page and on the notice board at the main gate.
Can I pay my maintenance fees online?
Currently, maintenance fees need to be paid at the management office in person. Online payments are being considered.
