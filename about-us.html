About Us

Welcome to the Avishikta Phase – 1 LIG (Type – A) Apartment Residents’ Welfare Association. We are a warm, vibrant community dedicated to nurturing the well-being, safety, and prosperity of all residents in our beautiful neighborhood located at Purbachal Kalitala Road, Kolkata.

Our association takes pride in fostering a spirit of unity and cooperation among members, encouraging active participation in cultural, social, and environmental initiatives that enhance our shared living experience. From organizing community events to advocating for sustainable living practices, we strive to build not just houses, but a welcoming home where every voice is valued and every resident feels connected.

We invite all members to join hands in shaping a future filled with harmony, mutual respect, and continuous growth — together creating a resilient and caring neighborhood for generations to come.

Thank you for being an integral part of our community journey at Avishikta Housing. Let’s continue to grow, support, and thrive as one family.
